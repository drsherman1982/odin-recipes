Spanish Omellette or Tortilla

The Tortilla is a great Spanish dish!!

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Olive oil
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Cook the potatoes,
  2. mix three eggs with the cooked potatoes,
  3. put butter and olive oil in the skillet and then put de mix
  4. cook the mix for 20 minutes each side
